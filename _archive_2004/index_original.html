Time that land forgot

High-bandwidth version

At the Locative workshop in Iceland Even Westvang and Timo Arnall collaborated on a project looking at ways of contextualising photographs by time and geography. This is our initial prototype.

There are two versions: a low-bandwidth no-image version and a high-bandwidth version with images. There is also a Quicktime movie for those unable run Flash at a reasonable frame rate.
